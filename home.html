NorthEast Women In Tech

NorthEastern Women In Tech (NEWIT) is a women-led start-up that aims at empowering women and girls with technological skills through mindset reengineering and core tech skills training.
Join our online community of like minded girls and women in Tech across the six northeastern states of Nigeria, to network, stay updated, get help and grow in your distinct tech career path.
