About Us

North East Women In Tech (NEWIT) is a women-led start-up that aims at empowering women and girls with technological skills through mindset reengineering and core tech skills training, to enable them earn and explore the limitless opportunities waiting to be harnessed, thereby eradicating poverty and reducing gender imbalance in the technological ecosystem. NEWIT also focuses on providing tech-related innovative and creative solutions to problems faced in Northeast Nigeria that can be tackled with tech-solutions.

Our Logo

Our Logo

Our logo speaks volume of who we are, our strengths, super powers and the core value that drives us. The lady face sitting pretty and bold shows how beautiful NortEastern women are, our resilience and boldness towards challanges. The tiny bits oozing out of the pretty lady face show our limitless potentials, grit and problem solving abilities, problems starting from our immediate communities to the world at large.
