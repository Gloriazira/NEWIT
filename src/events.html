Digital Marketing Training
Digital Marketing Training

This Digital Marketing Training is a three weeks training program sponsored by Northeast Women in Tech in collaboration with make IT here Tech and will be facilitated by a skilled professional in the Digital Marketing sphere. It is tailored towards equipping women with digital marketing skills. It is an immersive online experience, which allows participants to learn directly from experts.

The Possibilities In Tech
The Possibilities In Tech

The Possibilities in Tech Webinar Join us and @nickaldewereld a digital consultant and the founder of Make IT Here, to explore the possibilities in Tech and how to harness it especially as a young lady in Northeast Nigeria.

NEWIT Dairy Session
NEWIT Dairy Session

NEWIT DIARY SESSIONS (NDS) is a warm session where young like-minded ladies come together to share interesting stories about their journey in tech, challenges they have faced and how they approached and solved it. It's also a session where newbies transitioning into tech get answers and direct guidance from experts in the tech space. If you're a young woman in tech or just getting started, you might want to jump on this.
